## DUN Classification System

| Status | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Secure** | Strong position, no immediate threat | Maintain, monitor |
| **Favourable** | Leading, needs continued activity | Continue engagement |
| **Competitive** | Outcome genuinely contestable | Priority resources |
| **Vulnerable** | Deteriorating or opponent advantage | Emergency intervention |
| **Critical** | Immediate intervention necessary | War room activation |
| **Insufficient Intelligence** | Evidence inadequate | Intelligence collection priority |

---

## Machinery Readiness Rating

| Rating | Condition | Trigger |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| **Green** | Fully operational | - |
| **Amber** | Operational with material gaps | Monitor, reinforce |
| **Red** | Not ready / cannot meet minimum | Escalate immediately |
| **Grey** | Readiness cannot be verified | Deploy verification |

## CVS Framework (Core Truth Validation)

| Tier | Status | Confidence | Deployment |
|------|--------|------------|------------|
| **VERIFIED** | Multi-source (SPR + 2+ news) | 95%+ | Deploy-ready |
| **MEDIUM** | Single-source or inferred | 60-85% | Hold for verification |
| **EXCLUDED** | Unverified, pattern-inferred | <60% | Stripped from briefs |

## Escalation Triggers (Immediate)

- [ ] Projected governing pathway < 19 seats
- [ ] 3+ favourable DUNs move to competitive
- [ ] Critical DUN shows sustained deterioration (2 cycles)
- [ ] High-influence stakeholder changes position
- [ ] Negative narrative expands across multiple constituencies
- [ ] Competitive DUN rated Red for machinery
- [ ] Turnout below expected baseline
- [ ] Candidate rejection threatens electoral viability
- [ ] Data/intelligence materially inaccurate or compromised
- [ ] Misinformation achieves rapid statewide reach

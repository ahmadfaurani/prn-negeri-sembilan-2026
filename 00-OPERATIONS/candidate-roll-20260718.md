## 1. CRITICAL INTELLIGENCE FINDING

**🚨 ESC-011 (NEW — CRITICAL): BERSATU CONTESTING INDEPENDENTLY OF PN — OPPOSITION FOUR-WAY SPLIT CONFIRMED**

The SPR candidate list confirms that **BERSATU (24 candidates)** is contesting under its own party symbol, **separate from PN (11 candidates)**. The "BN-PN electoral pact" reported on 17 July was in fact a **BN-PAS pact** (PAS candidates running under the PN banner), while **Bersatu was excluded** and is running independently.

**Electoral configuration is now a FOUR-WAY split, not a two-bloc contest:**
- **PH:** 36 candidates (every DUN)
- **BN:** 25 candidates
- **BERSATU:** 24 candidates (independent of PN)
- **PN (PAS):** 11 candidates

**This directly contradicts the 17 July brief assessment** that "opposition unity achieved (ESC-006 RESOLVED)." The opposition is NOT unified. Bersatu's 24-candidate deployment creates a **three-way opposition split** in 21 of 36 DUNs, fundamentally changing the electoral calculus.

**Impact:**
- The BN-PN vote consolidation narrative is **invalid** for any DUN where BERSATU is also contesting
- PH benefits from opposition fragmentation, not suffers from consolidation
- The Muhyiddin-Samsuri friction (ESC-010) has **materialised into a formal electoral split**
- ESC-006 (Opposition Unity) must be **reopened and upgraded**

## Full text

IPOH: Prime Minister Datuk Seri Anwar Ibrahim has warned ministers, deputy ministers and heads of federal agencies to resign if they intend to use their federal positions to attack unity government partners during state election campaigns.Anwar said while political parties aligned at the federal level were free to contest against one another in state elections, those holding federal office must maintain discipline and avoid turning their positions into political platforms."If they wish to do so, they should first resign from their positions as ministers, deputy ministers or members of agency boards," he said.He said this to reporters after attending the Madani Kita Programme at Dewan Komuniti Taman Perpaduan here today.Anwar added that the reminder was conveyed during his discussions yesterday with Deputy Prime Ministers Datuk Seri Dr Ahmad Zahid Hamidi and Datuk Seri Fadillah Yusof, following the ongoing election campaign in Negri Sembilan.

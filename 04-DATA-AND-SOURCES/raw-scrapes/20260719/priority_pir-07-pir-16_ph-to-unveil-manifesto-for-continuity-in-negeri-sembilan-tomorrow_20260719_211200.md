(Bernama pic) SEREMBAN: Pakatan Harapan will unveil its manifesto for the 16th Negeri Sembilan state election tomorrow night. Negeri Sembilan PH operations director Zaki Sabri said the manifesto will focus on sustaining development and prosperity enjoyed by the people since PH took over the state government in 2018. Zaki said the manifesto has been holistically detailed by the coalition’s top leadership to ensure that sustainable economic and social impact for the people could be realised. “The manifesto will emphasise the continuity of the state’s success and progress as promised,” he said at a press conference here today. On party machinery efforts entering the second day of campaigning, Zaki said he was satisfied with the smooth logistics operations and the positive support received by PH candidates from local voters. A total of 889,490 registered voters are eligible to cast their ballots in the state election on Aug 1. They comprise 867,151 ordinary voters, and 16,884 military personnel and their spouses, as well as 5,455 police personnel as early voters.

SEREMBAN: Negri Sembilan police received one report related to the 16th state election yesterday, but no investigation paper was opened.

Negri Sembilan police chief Datuk Alzafny Ahmad said the overall security and public order situation throughout the election process remained under control.

"From the enforcement aspect, one police report was received yesterday, and no investigation paper regarding election offences was opened. The report involved a verbal dispute between party supporters," he said in a statement today.

He said police received 19 permit applications for political talks and campaign activities, all of which were approved after meeting the set conditions.

"All political programmes and activities held will be monitored by the police to ensure compliance with the law and to prevent any disruption to public safety and order," he said.

Alzafny said the state police contingent would continue to enhance its preparedness, monitoring, and comprehensive enforcement throughout the state election.

"Any violation of the law will be met with firm, fair, and integrity-driven action without compromise. Therefore, the public is urged to continue complying with the law, respect the democratic process, and cooperate fully with police on the ground to ensure the Negri Sembilan state election proceeds peacefully, harmoniously, and safely," he said.

He also advised the public against using social media platforms to upload, share, or spread statements that could incite hatred, enmity, or tension involving race, religion, the royal institution, or any parties involved in the election.

"We will not compromise with any individual found committing such offences. Firm action will be taken under the provisions of existing laws, including the Penal Code, the Communications and Multimedia Act 1998, the Sedition Act 1948, and other related laws," he said.

He advised the public to obtain information from authentic sources, avoid being easily influenced by provocations, and work together to safeguard public peace and national harmony.

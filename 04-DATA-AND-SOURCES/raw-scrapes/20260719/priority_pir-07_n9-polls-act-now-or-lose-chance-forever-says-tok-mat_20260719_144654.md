SEREMBAN: The understanding between Barisan Nasional (BN) and Perikatan Nasional (PN) in Negri Sembilan is an important effort to safeguard the future of race and religion, Umno deputy president Datuk Seri Mohamad Hasan said.He said the initiative was first mooted in 2018 but did not materialise."I reached an understanding that, to me, is very important for our race and religion. In Negri Sembilan, I started it in 2018, but somehow it did not materialise and fell through halfway."I said that if we don't do it this time, we will miss the boat. We will be disappointed because our children and grandchildren will become mere traders in their own country. I don't want that to happen," he said after attending the Sendayan Air Base Family Day Carnival today.Mohamad, who is also the Negri Sembilan BN chairman, said the effort must begin now, warning that failing to do so would mean losing the opportunity forever."If we don't start today, we will miss that opportunity forever. So in Negri Sembilan, I am fostering understanding to bring people together. This is for the future of our children and grandchildren, and let us all ensure this effort succeeds," he said.At the same event, Mohamad also recounted how the development of Sendayan, which had stalled due to an economic downturn, was eventually completed after he resolved compensation issues involving residents.Mohamad, who is also the BN candidate for the Rantau state seat, said the project has transformed Sendayan into one of the state's fastest-growing residential areas, attracting buyers from outside Negri Sembilan."Today, Sendayan has become what many describe as the Damansara of Seremban. Alhamdulillah, it is a development that I personally planned," he said.He said that every aspect of the township's development was carefully planned, including housing designs and public amenities, to ensure the comfort and well-being of residents.© New Straits Times Press (M) Bhd

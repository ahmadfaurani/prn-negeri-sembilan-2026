## Key Details

- **Event:** Majlis Makan Malam Perpaduan DUN Sikamat, Seremban
- **Speaker:** Perdana Menteri Datuk Seri Anwar Ibrahim
- **Subject:** PRN Negeri Sembilan — evaluation of MB Datuk Seri Aminuddin Harun's service record

---

## Key Quotes from PM Anwar Ibrahim

> "Demokrasi kita hormat tapi saya merayu kepada kawan-kawan kalau rekod Aminuddin ini, dia sombong di Sikamat, Ampangan, dia tak jaga tak peduli orang Melayu, Cina, India, harta dia, anak dia berbilion-bilion, saya beritahu jangan sokong dia."

> "Tapi kalau rekod dia, dia jaga kerja bukan sebab dia kawan saya, tapi dia menteri besar, kerja bertanggungjawab jadi berilah sokongan, saya minta itu sahaja, bagi sokongan, kami pun semangat bekerja."

> "Dengan izin Allah beliau akan terus menjadi Menteri Besar Negeri Sembilan. Kita ada calon, kita ada rekod."

> "Di sebelah sana, beberapa orang berebut, belum mampu umum lagi. Dari dulu dia berebut, sampai sekarang berebut jadi jangan beri mereka berebut."

---

## Summary of Main Points

- **Respect for democracy:** PM Anwar respects voters' decisions and the democratic process in PRN Negeri Sembilan, but urges rational decision-making.

- **Evaluate service record:** Voters should assess Aminuddin Harun's performance as Menteri Besar — if his record is poor, don't support him; if responsible and diligent, give support.

- **Aminuddin confirmed as MB candidate:** Anwar reiterated that Aminuddin will continue as the MB candidate for Negeri Sembilan, citing PH has both a candidate and a track record.

- **Contrast with opposition:** Anwar highlighted that the opposition side has multiple individuals competing for the MB position and has yet to announce their candidate, implying disunity.

- **Call for full PH support:** PM urged voters in Sikamat, Ampangan, and all DUN seats to give full support to Pakatan Harapan.

## Core Agenda: Intellectual Hub for Pertang

Jalaluddin aims to transform Pertang into an **intellectual hub** by nurturing more scholars among rural youth.

> *"Tentunya, saya tidak lari dalam soal untuk menaikkan ataupun menjadikan Pertang ini sebagai hab intelektual bagi melahirkan cendekiawan di kalangan anak-anak kampung."*

He noted that many young people in Pertang have the academic potential for higher education but are held back by **family economic pressures**, preventing them from accessing their rightful opportunities. This will be a **priority** for his next term.

---

## Key Focus Areas

### 1. Cost of Living
- Address rising cost of living, especially in **rural areas**.
- Boost community income through: **Agriculture sector**, **Small entrepreneurship**, **Rural economic activities**.

### 2. Internet Access in Rural Areas
- Critical for supporting **education** and **career needs** of residents.
- Jalaluddin has raised this issue in **Dewan Rakyat** covering: Jelebu and Pertang; all rural areas across Negeri Sembilan; **Orang Asli communities** statewide.
- Some areas have received internet transmitters but coverage remains **insufficient**.

> *"Isu ini tidak boleh tidak, mesti diselesaikan oleh pihak kerajaan, sama ada pihak kementerian dengan kerjasama kerajaan negeri, ia mesti dan wajar diselesaikan."*

### 3. Youth Development
Focus extends beyond sports and recreation facilities to include: **Education**, **Skills training**, **Employment opportunities**.

---

## Summary

Jalaluddin Alias, the BN incumbent for DUN Pertang, is campaigning on a platform of **education empowerment**, **economic upliftment of rural communities**, and **infrastructure improvement** (particularly internet access). His strategy combines his experience as both a former state assemblyman and current MP to address the dual challenges of rural poverty and limited educational access, with the overarching vision of making Pertang a hub for producing scholars from rural backgrounds.

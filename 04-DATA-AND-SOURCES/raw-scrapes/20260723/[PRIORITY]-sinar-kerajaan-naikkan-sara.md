## Key Announcement

Prime Minister Datuk Seri Anwar Ibrahim announced that the government will **increase Sumbangan Asas Rahmah (SARA) rates in the near future** to help reduce the cost of living burden on citizens.

## Context & Rationale

- The decision follows feedback and complaints from the public despite various forms of assistance already distributed
- Announcement made during **Ceramah Jelajah Kekal Harapan at Senawang**, in conjunction with the **Negeri Sembilan State Election (PRN) on 1 August**

## Key Quotes

> "Walaupun kita beri Sumbangan Tunai Rahmah (STR), SARA, (di Negeri Sembilan) Datuk Seri Aminuddin bagi bonus tinggi kepada penjawat awam, adakah selesai masalah? Belum."

> "Kerana itu, kita dengar rintihan rakyat dan insya-Allah dalam masa terdekat, kita akan naikkan lagi SARA untuk rakyat semua."

## Background & Future Plans

- The Madani Government will continue refining the possibility of increasing assistance amounts including STR and SARA if the country's economic situation permits
- Matter was discussed with Treasury Secretary-General Tan Sri Johan Mahmood Merican
- Will be considered in the Budget 2027 presentation

## Related Assistance Programs Mentioned

| Program | Status |
|---------|--------|
| **SARA** (Sumbangan Asas Rahmah) | To be increased in near future |
| **STR** (Sumbangan Tunai Rahmah) | Under consideration for increase |
| Negeri Sembilan public servant bonus | Already provided by Menteri Besar Datuk Seri Aminuddin |

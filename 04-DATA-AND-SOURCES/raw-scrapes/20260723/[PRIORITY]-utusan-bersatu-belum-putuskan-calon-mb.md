## Summary

Bersatu has not yet decided on its Menteri Besar candidate for the Negeri Sembilan State Election (PRN). This marks Bersatu's first time contesting elections under its own name. The announcement was made by party President Tan Sri Muhyiddin Yassin after the "Rapat Rakyat Bersatu Demi Negeri Sembilan" ceramah at Bilik Gerakan Utama DUN Sikamat, Seremban.

---

## Key Quotes from Muhyiddin Yassin

### On Menteri Besar Candidate:
> "Itu akan sampai masa nanti, kita tak perlu hendak umumkan apa-apa, kita tengok trend keadaan situasi macam mana, insya-Allah kalau sokongan kuat, kami yakin boleh dapat sejumlah kerusi mencukupi untuk membentuk kerajaan. Tentu ada jawatan menteri besar yang perlu diisi. Tetapi buat sementara kami tidak lagi putuskan apa-apa lah."

### On "King Maker" Possibility:
> "Walaupun di peringkat awal semasa hari penamaan calon nampak ada sambutan, ada tempat nampak meriah, tidaklah sepi, sunyi, itu petanda baik."

> "Tetapi pilihan raya ini kita boleh terlalu yakin, kita kena kerja keras, kempen mesti berjalan, jentera mesti licin, lancar dan sebagainya."

## Context

- Bersatu is monitoring current support trends and situation before making candidate decisions
- Early signs during nomination day showed positive reception in some areas
- Muhyiddin emphasized the need for hard work, smooth campaign machinery despite early positive indicators
- The party needs to secure sufficient seats to form government before considering the Menteri Besar position
- Event: Program Rapat Rakyat Bersatu Demi Negeri Sembilan at Bilik Gerakan Utama (BGU) DUN Sikamat, Seremban

## Summary of Key Points

### Main Assertion
- **PM Anwar Ibrahim** claims the real cause of early Negeri Sembilan state election (PRN) is **power struggle and the MB position**, not administrative failure of the state government.

### Political Stability & Economy
- Political stability is essential for economic growth.
- Political uncertainty causes investors to lose confidence; instability ultimately harms the people.

### Defense of MB Aminuddin Harun
- Throughout **3+ years** of Unity Government administration, no leadership complaints were raised about MB Aminuddin.
- No accusations that Aminuddin "only follows DAP's dictates."
- Current issues are about **power and who becomes MB** if certain coalitions win.
- State administration should be evaluated on: **service record, development, investment, efforts to solve people's problems** — not used as an excuse to grab power.

### PH Candidate Quality
- PH candidates described as "the best" based on track record, character (akhlak), commitment to the people.
- **Negeri Sembilan built the most new mosques** in a two-year period; additional social programs beyond mosque construction.

### Malay & Bumiputera Development
- Developing Malay/Bumiputera community requires **good and transparent governance**.
- Must also protect interests of Chinese and Indian communities.
- Government needs a clear agenda to uplift Malays; efforts should **not sacrifice the rights of other communities**.
- Anwar highlighted diverse cabinet: *"Saya dalam jemaah Menteri ada Mat Sabu, Anthony Loke."*

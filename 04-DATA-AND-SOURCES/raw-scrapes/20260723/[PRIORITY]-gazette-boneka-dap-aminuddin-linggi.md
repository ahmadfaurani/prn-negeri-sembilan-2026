## Main Arguments

### 1. Embracing the "Boneka DAP" Label
- Aminuddin **owns the criticism** that he is a "DAP puppet," turning it into a defense.
- Claims this so-called "puppet" has actually championed **Malay interests**.

### 2. Land Policy as Proof
- **Key policy achievement:** Converting land status from **leasehold → freehold**.
- Argues this primarily benefits **Malay homeowners** (since most leasehold property buyers are Malay).
- Effort was done with state government colleagues, then accepted by the Federal Government.

### 3. Inclusive Governance
- State administration does **not discriminate by race**.
- Ensures interests of Malays, Chinese, and Indians are all addressed fairly.
- Frames unity government as **teamwork**, not subservience to DAP.

### 4. PH-BN Cooperation & Stability
- **8 years of governance:** 5 years PH alone + 3 years PH-BN cooperation.
- Frames PRN as critical to **continuing political stability**.
- Urges voters to maintain the proven administration.

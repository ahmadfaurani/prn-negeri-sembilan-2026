  1. Datuk Muhammad Nazri Kassim  (PH-PKR)   — incumbent party's new face
  2. Noor'azah Harun             (BERSATU)   — splits anti-PH vote (Tier-4 PIR-06 overlap: Bersatu vs PAS)

  - 2008 (PRU): Pakatan Rakyat took it (thin majority 236)
  - 2018 (PRN): PH's Mohamad Rafie won (majority 1,306)

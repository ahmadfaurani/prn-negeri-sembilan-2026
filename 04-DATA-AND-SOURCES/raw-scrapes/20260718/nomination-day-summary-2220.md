## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY — WHAT CHANGED SINCE 21:03

Note: PH contests all 36 seats; Bersatu's 24 separate candidacies (running against both BN and PN) is the operational signature of the rupture.

Malek now contests for PN-PAS** — a PH→PN defection in the same seat. 3-cornered: Nazri (PH) vs Rafie (PN-PAS, ex-PH) vs Noor'azah (Bersatu). (Utusan, Seremban, 18 Jul 7:04pm)

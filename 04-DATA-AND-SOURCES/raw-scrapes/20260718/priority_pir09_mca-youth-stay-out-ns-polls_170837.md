MCA has allegedly instructed its youth wing secretary-general Saw Yee Fung to skip its campaign for the Negeri Sembilan state election, after she openly criticised BN's decision to cooperate with Perikatan Nasional.

Saw ( above ) revealed this in a lengthy Facebook post last night with a screenshot of a WhatsApp message from MCA secretary-general Chong Sin Woon that says "you don't have to come" for the state polls.

## Key Development

Early resistance from BN grassroots regarding the **BN-PN cooperation** in DUN Gemas is subsiding as the campaign enters its fourth day. Focus has shifted toward strengthening election machinery to ensure the alliance's victory.

## Key Quotes

> *"Peringkat awal memang saya tak nafikan, tetapi bila dah masuk empat hari(kempen) ni, Alhamdulillah dah mula kendur (kemarahan) dan nampak banyak perubahan."*
> — **Mohd Shukor Nasir**, UMNO Branch Chief Felda Jelai 4

> *"Saya hendak minta maaf kepada ahli-ahli UMNO dan saya faham mungkin pada awalnya ada yang sukar menerima keadaan ini. Tetapi saya berharap kita dapat ketepikan perbezaan dan bergerak sebagai satu pasukan demi memastikan kemenangan gabungan ini di DUN Gemas."*
> — **Ridzuan Ahmad**, PN Candidate & Incumbent for DUN Gemas

## Summary of Key Points

### Grassroots Response
- **Mohd Shukor Nasir** (also PDM Chief for Felda Jelai 2 and 4) dismissed boycott claims as **unfounded**
- Initial reactions were described as **normal** — members needed time to adjust to the new political landscape
- Grassroots now understand the decision was made by party leadership for a **greater objective**
- Shukor, a veteran politician, described his role as providing "air sejuk" (cooling comfort) to members
- Machinery from both parties now seen **moving in tandem**

### Candidate Profile: Ridzuan Ahmad
- **Incumbent** for DUN Gemas, won in the last PRN on a **Bersatu/PN ticket**
- **Last election result:** 11,653 votes, winning with a **majority of 3,120** over BN's Abd. Razak Ab. Said
- Recently **left Bersatu and joined Parti Wawasan (WAWASAN)**
- Still selected to represent **PN** to defend the seat this election
- Acknowledged the cooperation was **not easy for all parties** initially
- Reports improving relations between BN and PN machinery during campaign

### Current Contestants for DUN Gemas
| Candidate | Party/Coalition |
|---|---|
| **Ridzuan Ahmad** (Incumbent) | PN (via WAWASAN) |
| **Azman Abdullah** | Bersatu |
| **Siti Aishah Seman @ Othman** | Pakatan Harapan (PH) |

## Broader Significance

The development signals that **early concerns about BN grassroots acceptance** of the BN-PN alliance in Gemas are **fading**, with both parties' machinery now cooperating ahead of polling day in the Negeri Sembilan state election.

## PIR-06 Assessment
Ridzuan Ahmad is the former Gemas assemblyman who quit Bersatu (previously flagged 11 Jul). He now defends the seat under PN/Wawasan. Bersatu's Azman Abdullah creates a 3-cornered fight. The BN grassroots anger over the BN-PN pact (giving Gemas to a former Bersatu/PN incumbent) is subsiding — confirms machinery coordination is operationalising despite initial friction. This is a consequence-of-split data point: Bersatu's solo candidate (Azman) is the opposition to the BN-PN cooperative incumbent.

## PIR-07 Assessment
DUN Gemas (N.36) — 3-cornered: PN/Wawasan (Ridzuan, incumbent) vs Bersatu (Azman Abdullah) vs PH (Siti Aishah). Grassroots sentiment tracking: BN anger fading by day 4 of campaign. Ridzuan won 11,653 votes (majority 3,120) last cycle.

PETALING JAYA: Police have arrested 12 people, including a 15-year-old boy, over a brawl at a restaurant in Taman Daya, Johor Bahru, early this morning.
Johor police chief Ab Rahaman Arsad said police were informed of the incident at 5.40am and arrested the suspects between 10am and 11am.
“All the suspects were arrested around the Taman Setia Indah area not long after the incident was reported.
“Checks found that three of the suspects have criminal records,” Utusan Malaysia reported him as saying.
A two-minute, 36-second video circulating on social media shows the brawl, which is believed to have started during the screening of the World Cup final between Spain and Argentina.
Rahaman said the suspects would be brought to the Johor Bahru magistrates’ court tomorrow for a remand application to assist in the investigation under Section 148 of the Penal Code for rioting with weapons.

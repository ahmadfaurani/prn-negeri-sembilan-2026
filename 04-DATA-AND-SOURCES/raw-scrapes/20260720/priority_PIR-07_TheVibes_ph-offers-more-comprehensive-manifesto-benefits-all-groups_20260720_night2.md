THE Negeri Sembilan Pakatan Harapan (PH) manifesto to be launched tonight will feature various initiatives for all levels of society in the state.
Negeri Sembilan PH chairman, Datuk Seri Aminuddin Harun, said PH wants the manifesto offered in conjunction with the Negeri Sembilan State Election (PRN) to benefit all groups.
“God willing, it will not only be for the B40 group, but we will also cater to the M40. We want more people to benefit from the programs and initiatives offered,” he said.
The Negeri Sembilan PH manifesto is scheduled to be launched tonight, which will also be attended by several key party leaders.
The manifesto has been holistically detailed by the coalition’s top leadership to ensure that a sustainable economic and social impact for the people could be realised.
Meanwhile, Aminuddin said that many initiatives introduced by the previous state government will also be maintained in the manifesto.
“We are continuing many of them; we have received information that previous initiatives have provided great benefits to the people of Negeri Sembilan.
“There are additional initiatives, but more importantly, we do not want them to be limited to the B40 group,” he said. – July 20, 2026

PRN Negeri Sembilan: Jalaluddin, Anthony Loke arrive a nomination centre
The four candidates are Anthony Loke Siew Fook (Chennah), Mohd Umry Abdul Khois (Pertang), Zainal Fikri Abd Kadir (Sungai Lui) and Bakri Sawir (Klawang).
Updated 2 days ago · Published on 18 Jul 2026 9:37AM
The arrival of the candidates and key leaders was also greeted with drum beats by PH supporters. - July 18, 2026
FOUR Pakatan Harapan (PH) candidates for the Chennah, Pertang, Sungai Lui and Klawang State Legislative Assembly (DUN) seats arrived at the Kuala Klawang Grand Hall for the nomination process for the Negeri Sembilan State Election (PRN).
The four candidates are Anthony Loke Siew Fook (Chennah), Mohd Umry Abdul Khois (Pertang), Zainal Fikri Abd Kadir (Sungai Lui) and Bakri Sawir (Klawang).
They arrived at approximately 8:47 am, accompanied by several key PH leaders, including Amanah President Datuk Seri Mohamad Sabu, DAP Advisor Lim Guan Eng, and National DAP Chairman Gobind Singh Deo.
The arrival of the candidates and key leaders was also greeted with drum beats by PH supporters.
Meanwhile, three candidates from Barisan Nasional (BN) arrived shortly afterwards, at about 8.55 am.
The three candidates are Datuk Seri Jalaluddin Alias ​​(Pertang), Siow Kong Choon (Chennah) and Datuk Mohd Razi Mohd Ali (Sungai Lui).
UMNO Vice-President, Datuk Seri Wan Rosdy Wan Ismail, accompanied all BN candidates. – July 18, 2026

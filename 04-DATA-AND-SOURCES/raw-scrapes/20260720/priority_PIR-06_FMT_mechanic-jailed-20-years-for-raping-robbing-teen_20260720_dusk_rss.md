PETALING JAYA: A mechanic was sentenced to 20 years’ imprisonment and seven strokes of the cane by the sessions court in Pasir Mas today after pleading guilty to raping and robbing a 15-year-old girl in Tumpat last month.
Faryzuan Aiman Mat Yatim, 23, pleaded guilty to both charges before judge Zulkipli Abdullah, according to Berita Harian.
He was charged with raping and injuring the teenager in a bushy area in Kampung Mesra Palekbang, Tumpat, at about 7.40am on June 17.
The charge was framed under Section 376(2)(a) of the Penal Code, which provides for up to 20 years’ imprisonment and whipping.
He was also accused of robbing the victim of her Vivo Y19 mobile phone at the same location while armed with a wooden stick.
The charge was framed under Section 392 of the Penal Code, which carries a jail term of up to 14 years with the possibility of a fine or whipping.
Deputy public prosecutor Siti Aisyha Nai’lah Harizan urged the court to impose a deterrent sentence, citing public interest and the gravity of the offences.
She said Faryzuan’s actions were cruel, and had caused the victim severe trauma and fear, in addition to serious physical injuries.
Faryzuan, who was unrepresented, pleaded for a lighter sentence, saying he has a wife and a three-year-old child.
The court sentenced him to 15 years’ imprisonment and five strokes of the cane for rape, and five years’ imprisonment and two strokes of the cane for robbery.
The sentences are to run consecutively, beginning from the date of his arrest on July 7.
The court also ordered him to undergo counselling throughout his imprisonment and remain under police supervision for three years after completing his sentence.

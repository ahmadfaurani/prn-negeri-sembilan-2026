NS PRN: PH administration's success, Aminuddin's ability will be campaign focus - Fahmi
Fahmi said the various changes brought by Aminudin to the people in the state were PH's strength to win the mandate.
Updated 2 days ago · Published on 18 Jul 2026 2:43PM
"All campaigns, whether physical or online, avoid slander and the 3R issue," said Fahmi - July 18, 2026
THE continuity of the Pakatan Harapan (PH) administration in Negeri Sembilan and the excellent performance of Menteri Besar, Datuk Seri Aminudin Harun are among the campaign focuses in the Negeri Sembilan State Election (PRN).
Pakatan Harapan (PH) Communications Director, Datuk Seri Fahmi Fadzil, said the various changes brought by Aminudin to the people in the state were PH's strength to win the mandate.
"For PH, our focus is on the success and capabilities of the Menteri Besar (Aminuddin) who has implemented many changes and improvements in terms of the economy, zakat collection and development.
"I believe in the stability he has brought, no scandals, no issues, no problems throughout his eight years of administration.
"I see this as something we need to continue so that there is continuity and a close relationship between the state and the federation. This will be among the things we will campaign for throughout the PRN," he said.
Asked about the cooperation between Perikatan Nasional (PN) and Barisan Nasional (BN), Fahmi said that it was not an obstacle to defending the state under PH administration.
"The election machinery is ready to defend the PH government in Negeri Sembilan, I see the situation under control and I hope it remains so for the next two weeks.
"All campaigns, whether physical or online, avoid slander and the 3R issue... we hope everything goes well," he added. – July 18, 2026

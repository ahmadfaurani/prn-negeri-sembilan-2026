PETALING JAYA: A man putting up political party flags for the Negeri Sembilan state election was killed in a hit-and-run accident near Seremban yesterday.
Seremban police chief Yatim Osman said the crash occurred along Jalan Kuala Sawah-Rantau at about 6.40pm.
In a statement, Yatim said an investigation found that the man was working on the shoulder of the road in the direction of Rantau when he was struck.
He said a lorry is believed to have first hit a signpost before crashing into the man.
The victim suffered injuries to his face and head and was pronounced dead at the scene.
The the driver is believed to have fled the scene following the crash.
The lorry’s type and registration number have yet to be established.

# Setiap Parti Memang Letak Sasaran Menang – Jalaluddin

**Source:** Utusan Malaysia | **Date:** 22 July 2026 | **Author:** Fatin Norizati Mat Isa
**URL:** https://www.utusan.com.my/nasional/2026/07/setiap-parti-memang-letak-sasaran-menang-jalaluddin/

---

## Key Quote
> "Apabila bertanding, tentunya kena yakin. Walaupun sasaran itu terlalu baik untuk dipercayai, ia harus berasaskan realiti. Kita tunggu dan lihat sama ada mereka atau Barisan Nasional (BN) yang dapat sekurang-kurangnya 23 kerusi." — Datuk Seri Jalaluddin Alias

## Summary

### Jalaluddin's Statement
- Every contesting party sets its own winning targets and confidence levels
- Targets must be based on **current reality**, including **grassroots conditions**
- Made during visit to Felda Pasoh 1, Simpang Pertang, Jelebu

### PH's Seat Targets (Negeri Sembilan PRN)
- **22-24 DUN seats** targeted to form government with **two-thirds majority**
- **Anthony Loke** (Timbalan Pengerusi PH): PH needs at least **23 of 36 DUN seats**
- Larger mandate needed to ensure **stable state government administration**
- **Aminuddin Harun** chose to contest in **DUN Linggi** to help PH reach target

### Government Formation Thresholds
| Threshold | Seats Required |
|-----------|----------------|
| Simple majority | 19 seats |
| Two-thirds majority | 24 seats |

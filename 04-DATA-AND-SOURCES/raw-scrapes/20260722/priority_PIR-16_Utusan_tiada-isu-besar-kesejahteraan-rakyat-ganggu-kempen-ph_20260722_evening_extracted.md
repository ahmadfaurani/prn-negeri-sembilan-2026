# Tiada Isu Besar Kesejahteraan Rakyat Ganggu Kempen PH

**Source:** Utusan Malaysia | **Date:** 22 Julai 2026, 4:28 pm | **Author:** Badrul Hafizan Mat Isa
**URL:** https://www.utusan.com.my/nasional/2026/07/tiada-isu-besar-kesejahteraan-rakyat-ganggu-kempen-ph/
**PIR Tags:** PIR-16 (Campaign Narrative — PH campaign assessment, Linggi)

---

## Key Points

- **No major issues** regarding state development or people's welfare are plaguing PH's campaign during PRN Negeri Sembilan
- PH Negeri Sembilan Chairman **Datuk Seri Aminuddin Harun** (also PH candidate for Linggi) stated that people's problems have been addressed early by the state government
- Issues such as **land disputes** and **local community matters** have already been resolved
- Only **minor issues** are being raised during the campaign:

> "Biasa yang kami dengar hanya masalah jalan, masalah longkang, masalah monyet yang banyak di kampung-kampung mengganggu masyarakat untuk bercucuk tanam. Tidak ada isu yang besar, tidak ada isu-isu yang terkait dengan kerajaan buat masa ni."

## JHEAINS Teaching License Suspension
- Aminuddin **supports** the decision by JHEAINS to temporarily suspend religious teaching licenses for politicians and candidates during the PRN campaign period
- He believes this step **should have been implemented earlier**

> "Kita tidak mahu masjid dan surau dijadikan medan untuk mengajar agama sambil berkempen dalam pilihan raya."

## Context
- Aminuddin made these statements after attending a **Ziarah Kasih** (Compassionate Visit) program at Rumah Rakyat Permatang Pasir, Linggi, Port Dickson
- The visit included meeting with a sick resident at the public housing area

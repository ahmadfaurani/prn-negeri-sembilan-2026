## Key Excerpts

> "Apabila bertemu pengundi, mereka meluahkan rasa syukur kerana menerima STR dan SARA. Bantuan ini benar-benar dekat dengan hati rakyat kerana mereka boleh terus membeli barangan keperluan."

> "Saya yakin kehadiran PMX malam ini akan memberi suntikan semangat kepada jentera dan melonjakkan lagi sokongan kepada Pakatan Harapan khususnya di DUN Sikamat."

> "Ramai mengenali saya kerana pernah menjadi imam solat, menguruskan solat jenazah ahli keluarga mereka atau menikahkan anak-anak mereka. Saya juga sering mengisi kuliah agama di surau dan masjid sekitar Sikamat. Sebab itu ramai mengenali saya sebagai guru."

## Context
- **Election:** PRN Negeri Sembilan ke-16
- **Polling day:** 1 Ogos 2026
- **Constituency:** N.13 Sikamat
- **Candidate:** Nor Azman Mohamad (Pakatan Harapan)
- **Campaign day:** 5th day (22 July 2026)

## PM's Visit Expected to Boost Support
- PM Datuk Seri Anwar Ibrahim scheduled to attend **Makan Malam Perpaduan** (Unity Dinner) in Sikamat tonight
- Nor Azman believes PM's presence will **boost voter support** and **motivate campaign machinery**

## Voter Feedback on Government Aid
- Voters expressing gratitude for federal government assistance:
  - **STR** (Sumbangan Tunai Rahmah) — cash aid
  - **SARA** (Sumbangan Asas Rahmah) — basic necessities aid
- Aid credited via **MyKad** — allows direct purchase of essential goods

## Campaign Activities (Day 5)
1. Breakfast with residents near **Balai Raya Desa Rhu**
2. Walkabout session at **Kampung Ujong Pasir**
3. House-to-house visits around Sikamat
4. Afternoon program at **Paroi Jaya**
5. Distributing manifesto leaflets to voters

## Sikamat's Political History
- Sikamat has **consistent support for PH since 2008**
- Nor Azman hopes to **maintain public confidence** in the government

## Nor Azman's Background & Strengths
- Served as **imam and preacher** in Sikamat area for over a decade
- Was imam at **Masjid Jamek Datuk Kelana Petra Lela** (2004-2011)
- Well-known locally for leading prayers, managing funeral prayers, solemnizing marriages, conducting religious classes

## If Elected — Priorities
- Welfare (kebajikan)
- Education programs (pendidikan)
- Human development (pembangunan insan)
- Activities for elderly (warga emas) and youth (belia)

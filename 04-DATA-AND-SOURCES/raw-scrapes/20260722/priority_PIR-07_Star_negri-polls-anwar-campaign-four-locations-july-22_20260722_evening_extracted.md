# Negri Polls: Anwar to Campaign at Four Locations on July 22

**Source:** The Star (Bernama) | **Published:** Tuesday, 21 Jul 2026, 11:33 PM MYT
**URL:** https://www.thestar.com.my/news/nation/2026/07/21/negri-polls-anwar-to-campaign-at-four-locations-on-july-22

## Key Details

- **Event:** 16th Negri Sembilan State Election campaign
- **Principal Figure:** Prime Minister Datuk Seri Anwar Ibrahim (also Pakatan Harapan chairman)
- **Significance:** This marks Anwar's **first visit** to Negri Sembilan since the state election campaign began.

## Campaign Itinerary — Wednesday, July 22

| Time | Event | Location |
|------|-------|----------|
| **4:00 PM** | Santai Petang Bersama Warga Ampangan | Benteng @ Medan Selera Ampangan |
| **5:00 PM** | Jelajah Rakan Muda | Negri Sembilan Youth and Sports Complex, Paroi |
| **8:00 PM** | Sikamat State Legislative Assembly Unity Dinner | Taman Sri Penaga |
| **Evening** | Paroi State Legislative Assembly Pakatan Harapan Grand Rally | Taipan 2, Senawang Town Centre |

## Election Schedule
- **Early Voting:** July 28
- **Polling Day:** August 1

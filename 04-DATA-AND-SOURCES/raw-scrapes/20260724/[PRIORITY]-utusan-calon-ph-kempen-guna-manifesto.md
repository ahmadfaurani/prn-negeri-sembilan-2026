## Key Development

Pakatan Harapan (PH) akan memastikan semua calon yang bertanding memberi tumpuan kepada penyampaian manifesto sepanjang kempen PRN Negeri Sembilan berbanding menggunakan retorik atau menyerang pihak lawan.

## Key Figure

- **Datuk Seri Aminuddin Harun** — Pengerusi PH Negeri Sembilan & Menteri Besar

## Event Context

- **Acara:** Sidang media selepas Majlis Peluncuran Manifesto "Kekal Harapan" di Seremban

## Core Points

1. **All PH candidates** instructed to focus on delivering manifesto content during campaign
2. Candidates must **fully understand** the manifesto before presenting it to voters
3. Campaign should explain **plans and offers** to be implemented if given mandate — not slander or unbeneficial matters
4. PH offering a **"realistic and implementable"** manifesto

## Key Quotes — Aminuddin Harun

> "Kita akan pastikan calon-calon sentiasa membaca, memahami dan menyampaikan kepada rakyat apa yang ingin dilaksanakan menerusi manifesto."

> "Kita tidak mahu berkempen dengan fitnah, bergaduh atau membangkitkan perkara yang tidak perlu. Sebaliknya, kita mahu menawarkan manifesto yang realistik dan boleh dilaksanakan."

> "Kami bukan kerajaan negeri yang baharu mentadbir. Sudah lapan tahun kami memerintah sejak 2018 dan kami yakin mampu melaksanakan segala yang dijanjikan kepada rakyat."

## Manifesto Details

- **Theme:** "Negeri Sembilan Kekal Harapan"
- Contains **10 main commitments** (10 komitmen utama)
- Launched by Aminuddin Harun

## Candidate Information

- **Aminuddin Harun** contesting **Linggi DUN seat** in this PRN
- PH has **8 years of administration record** (since 2018) as backing for their promises

## Related News (23 July 2026)

- MCMC detected two fake accounts, 14 network complaints during PRN Negeri Sembilan
- BN focused on winning PRN, has not decided MB candidate
- DAP increasingly concerned about BN-PN cooperation
- PH maintains focus on manifesto, refuses to engage in slander campaigns

## PIR-16 Significance

- **PH campaign strategy directive:** manifesto-focused, anti-retorik, anti-slander
- Contrasts with BN-PN's more combative approach (Annuar Musa "kuasa untuk laksana", Hamzah's SARA-timing attacks)
- "8 years track record" = credibility play — PH has governing experience to back promises
- "Realistik dan boleh dilaksanakan" = implicit critique of opponents' promises (BN 50k jobs, Bersatu pending manifesto)
- This strategy aligns with voter sentiment (Malay Mail Day 6 survey: voters want "leaders who can get things done", reject negative campaigning)

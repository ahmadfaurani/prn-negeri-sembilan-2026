## Executive Summary

Campaign Day 7 (morning cycle) saw the **BN-PN alliance shift from narrative defense to offensive mega-ceramah deployment**, launching two joint "Ceramah Umum Perdana" events on 24 July — one at Parlimen Jempol (covering N.05–N.08) featuring PAS Election Director Sanusi Md Nor, and one at Parlimen Rembau (covering N.21–N.26) featuring Hamidah. This represents the first major joint BN-PN mega-ceramah surge of the campaign and signals a coordinated machinery deployment across two parliamentary zones simultaneously. The Jempol ceramah produced the cycle's most striking CT-07 development: **Sanusi claimed "Bersatu menyerang PAS lebih daripada PH"** (Bersatu attacks PAS more than PH), exposing internal coalition friction between Bersatu and PAS within the PN-BN alliance. At the Rembau ceramah, Hamidah prayed for Bersatu to be "dibukakan hati" (have their hearts opened), further signaling tension in the cooperation.

The narrative battle escalated on two fronts. First, **Annuar Musa launched a direct counter-attack on PH's "penyatuan ummah" critique** from the previous cycle, flipping the "asabiyah" (religious exclusivism) label back onto PH, claiming "PH lebih asabiyah berbanding PN." This neutralizes PH's offensive and turns the same religious vocabulary against the accuser. Second, **Tok Mat attacked PH's two-term record as "potong reben sahaja"** (just ribbon-cutting), demanding NS development be "restored to BN era levels." A new analytical narrative also emerged this cycle: the **"isu istana" (palace issue)** — two university professors (Jayum Jawan of UiTM, Azizuddin Sani of UUM) independently identified the circumstances of the early dissolution (loss of majority, palace role) as a key campaign determinant, alongside candidate quality and party logo strength.

On the ground, **Fahmi Fadzil's visit to DUN Jeram Padang (N.07)** with PH candidate Manivannan breaks that critical 4-cornered DUN's three-cycle zero-activity streak — a significant CT-01 escalation resolution. The N.33 Sri Tanjung BEBAS candidate facing Section 233 CMA charges is now **CONFIRMED as Islah Wahyudi Zainudin (Melayu, 46)**, resolving the previous [UNVERIFIED] tag. The BN manifesto launch, anticipated at Linggi today (24 Jul), was previewed extensively overnight with Jalaluddin emphasizing "kestabilan tatasusila, pentadbiran adat dan Raja-Raja Melayu" — a direct play on NS's unique constitutional structure. Separately, **Hamzah Zainudin announced the formation of a new party, "Parti Wawasan Negara,"** adding a new variable to coalition dynamics.

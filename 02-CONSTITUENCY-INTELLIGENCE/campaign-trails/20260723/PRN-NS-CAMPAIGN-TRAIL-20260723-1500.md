## Executive Summary

Campaign Day 5 saw the highest-intensity day of the PRN NS 2026 trail so far, with **PM Anwar Ibrahim's first NS campaign tour** (4 locations: Ampangan, Paroi, Sikamat, Senawang) dominating coverage, while **Muhyiddin Yassin's concurrent Sikamat ceramah** created a direct PH-vs-Bersatu visibility clash at the same DUN on the same night. The cycle's most significant new development is the **escalation of election-offence investigations** from 5 to **7 kertas siasatan** (with 12 total police reports), including 2 new Section 427 mischief cases opened on 22 Jul. Police also confirmed **226 cumulative ceramah permits** approved (up from 173).

On the machinery front, **DPM Zahid Hamidi publicly expressed dissatisfaction** with BN's campaign machinery ("saya belum puas hati"), ordering all 25 BN candidates to intensify operations and positioning BN as the "underdog." This is a significant CT-08 signal — the BN chairman is publicly acknowledging weak ground operations on Day 4. Separately, **Danni Rais (PN, N.04 Klawang) confirmed his machinery issues with BN are resolved** — 16 UMNO branches that initially didn't support him are now cooperating, with PDM workers alternating PN/BN vests.

BN announced its manifesto will launch **24 July at DUN Linggi** with a headline pledge of **50,000 jobs in 5 years** (vs PH's 30,000). Jalaluddin Alias (BN, N.02 Pertang) explicitly admitted at a Jelebu ceramah that "BN sukar tawan NS tanpa PN" — a candid acknowledgment of coalition dependency. Aminuddin Harun (PH, N.32 Linggi) mounted a vigorous defense against the "boneka DAP" label, citing freehold land conversion policy as proof of Malay welfare advocacy.
